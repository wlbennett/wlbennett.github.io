Applications of Dance

There are endless possibilities for the usage of dance, here are a couple...

Learn More

Therapeutic Dance

Used in dance therapy, it focuses on the health and emotional benefits of dance. It can help in improving mental health, physical fitness, and emotional expression.

Watch

Competitive Dance

Involves dancers competing against each other in various styles, often judged on technique, expression, and choreography. It includes formats like ballroom competitions, street dance battles, and dance sport events.

Watch

Commercial Dance

Used in entertainment industries such as movies, television, and concerts. It's often seen in music videos, commercials, and live shows, often designed to appeal to a wide audience.

Watch

Cultural Dance

Represents the dance forms that are specific to a particular culture or region. These dances are often rooted in the history, folklore, and traditions of a community and are performed during cultural festivals, rituals, and celebrations.

Watch

Educational Dance

Utilized in academic settings, it focuses on teaching the art of dance, including its history, techniques, and creative expression. Educational dance can be a part of school curricula or special dance academies and workshops.

Watch

Social Dance

A form of recreational dance that is primarily about socializing and enjoyment. It's less about technique and more about participation and interaction. Examples include dances like salsa, swing, and ballroom dancing at social gatherings.
